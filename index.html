About The Event

Join us for an unforgettable automotive experience in the beautiful hills of Ranikhet, Almora. Whether you're a car enthusiast or a bike lover, you'll find something to spark your passion. Explore the latest models, classic rides, thrilling stunts, and enjoy activities for all ages!

  • Supercar & superbike showcases
  • Live drag races & stunt shows
  • Test drives & demo rides
  • Food trucks, music & entertainment
  • Photo booths, contests & exclusive giveaways
