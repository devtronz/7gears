About The Event

Be part of the most exhilarating automotive gathering of 2025! On 25th February 2025, Central Park transforms into a paradise for car and bike lovers. From supercars and classic bikes to roaring drag races and family-friendly fun, discover the world of wheels like never before.

  • Supercar & superbike showcases
  • Live drag races & stunt shows
  • Test drives & demo rides
  • Food trucks, music & entertainment
  • Photo booths, contests & exclusive giveaways
